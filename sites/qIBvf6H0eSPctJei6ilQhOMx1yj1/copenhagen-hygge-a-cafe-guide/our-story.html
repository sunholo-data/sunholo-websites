Behind the Steam

A gentle invitation to slow down and enjoy a warm cup in a cozy Danish corner. Our journey began with a simple love for the scent of freshly ground beans and the soft glow of candlelight.

Hand-Drawn Memories

We started this guide as a collection of sketches and napkins notes. Every cafe we recommend has been visited, sketched, and loved by our small team of locals. We believe that the best recommendations come from genuine moments of connection.

Our goal is to share that specific feeling of Danish "Hygge" that can only be found when the world slows down for a perfect espresso.

What We Look For

Local Soul

We prioritize local ingredients and cafes that support the community of Copenhagen farmers and producers.

Eco-Roasting

Sustainable roasting practices are non-negotiable. We look for beans that are ethically sourced and roasted with care.

The 'Home' Feel

That unmistakable feeling of 'home' away from home—where the lighting is low and the welcome is warm.
