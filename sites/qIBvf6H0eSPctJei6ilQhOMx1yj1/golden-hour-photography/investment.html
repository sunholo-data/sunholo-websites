Common Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your session.

What is your turnaround time?

Landscape prints ship within 5 days. Wedding galleries are delivered within 6-8 weeks, with a "sneak peek" gallery sent within 48 hours of your celebration.

Do you travel for weddings?

Absolutely. I love documenting love stories in new and inspiring locations. Travel fees are calculated based on distance and accommodation needs, always kept as transparent as possible.

How do we secure our date?

A signed contract and a 30% non-refundable retainer are required to officially reserve your date on my calendar.
