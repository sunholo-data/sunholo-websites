About Us

Our journey and legacy in South West Cornwall

Our History

SW Cornwall Group was formed in 1976. Over the years, our riders have competed at National levels with great success. What started as a small initiative has blossomed into a vital community resource for riders throughout the region.

Thanks to major fundraising and the dedication of our community, we now enjoy a covered school at Old Mill Stables, ensuring sessions run regardless of the Cornish weather. This facility allows us to provide consistent, year-round therapy and joy to those who need it most.

From Our Trustees

"Volunteering for RDA is very rewarding. Being part of the sessions, watching the enjoyment on the children’s faces and seeing them progress both physically and behaviourally makes it a worthwhile commitment."
— Anthea Carr, Group Secretary & Trustee
