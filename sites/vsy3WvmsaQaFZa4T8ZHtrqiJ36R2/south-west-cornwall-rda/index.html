Countless Smiles

A Legacy of Care

Founded to provide horse riding for disabled children from Nancealverne Special School, our group has grown to host annual holidays, regional competitions, and even a royal visit from Princess Anne.

Over nearly five decades, we have remained true to our mission: providing therapy, fitness, and skill development through the unique bond between horse and rider.
