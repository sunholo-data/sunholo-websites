The Covered School Project

Our major fundraising campaign allowed us to erect a covered school, meaning no more cancelled sessions due to weather. Your donations directly impact our facilities and ensure our riders get the consistency they need.

This achievement stands as a testament to the incredible generosity of our local South West Cornwall community and the hard work of our dedicated fundraisers.
