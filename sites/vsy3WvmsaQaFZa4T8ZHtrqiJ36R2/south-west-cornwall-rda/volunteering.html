Volunteers are the Heart of RDA

Without our committed volunteers, we could not provide such a brilliant experience for our riders. Join our team today and make a real difference in your community.

Ways to Help

No horse experience? No problem. We provide full training for all our roles.

Sidewalking

Interacting with riders and ensuring their safety and confidence during sessions.

Pony Leading

Focusing on the pony's movement and guidance to ensure a smooth ride.

Administration

Helping with registers, forms, and the vital organization that keeps the group running.

Fundraising

Assisting with coffee mornings, dog shows, and country fairs to raise essential funds.
