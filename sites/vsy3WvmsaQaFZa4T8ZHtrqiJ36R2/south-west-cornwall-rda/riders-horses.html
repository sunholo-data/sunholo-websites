Therapy in Motion

RDA can improve physical health, mobility, and mental wellbeing. We've seen students progress from being unable to maintain body weight to holding themselves upright unaided.

The rhythmic movement of the horse mimics the human walk, helping to strengthen core muscles and improve balance in a way that traditional physiotherapy often cannot replicate.

Our Programs

Tailored sessions for every need, focusing on ability rather than disability.

Riding Sessions

Therapeutic riding held every Wednesday at Old Mill Stables. Our qualified instructors ensure safety and progress for every rider.

Tea with a Pony

A sensory experience for those who may not want to ride but seek the calming influence of horses. Perfect for people living with dementia or social anxiety.

Competitions

Opportunities to compete at County, Regional, and National levels, building confidence and a sense of achievement.

A Rider's Journey: Jake & Max

"I learned what I could do, having never thought I'd ride again. My confidence leapt as I trained to compete. Max is a grounding consistency in my life. RDA made that possible."

Jake's story is one of many. Through the partnership with horses like Max, our riders find new strengths and rediscover their independence in a supportive environment.
