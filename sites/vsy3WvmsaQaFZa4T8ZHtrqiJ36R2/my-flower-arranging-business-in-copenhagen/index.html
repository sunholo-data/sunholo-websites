The Studio

Rooted in
Copenhagen

Blomst Studio was born from a deep love of flowers and a belief that a thoughtfully arranged bouquet can change the feeling of a space — and a moment.

We work with seasonal, locally sourced flowers and foliage, letting the natural textures and colours of each stem guide the composition. No two arrangements are alike.

From a single statement vase for your home to full floral styling for a wedding or event — we bring the same care and attention to every order.

What we offer

Flowers for every
occasion

Whether you are planning a wedding, a dinner party, or simply want flowers in your home every week — we have something for you.

01

Weddings & Events

Full floral styling for your wedding, dinner, or celebration. We work closely with you from concept to creation, designing arrangements that reflect the mood and spirit of your event.

02

Bespoke Bouquets

A one-of-a-kind arrangement made to order. Perfect as a gift or for your own home. Tell us your colours, mood, or occasion — and we'll do the rest.

03

Weekly Subscriptions

Fresh flowers delivered to your door each week. Choose your size and colour palette, and enjoy a seasonal arrangement that changes with the market.

04

Corporate & Offices

Regular floral installations for workplaces, restaurants, and retail spaces. We handle everything — delivery, setup, and weekly refresh.

05

Workshops

Learn the fundamentals of flower arranging in an intimate group session. A wonderful afternoon with friends, colleagues, or as a solo creative experience.

06

Sympathy & Ceremony

Respectful, beautiful arrangements for funerals and memorial services. We approach each commission with care, sensitivity, and an eye for quiet beauty.
