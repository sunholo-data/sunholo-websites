Always something on

Live Music & Events

From Tuesday open-mic nights to the big Christmas party, there's always a reason to come in. All ages, all welcome.

Every week

Live music nights

We host live music throughout the week — from intimate acoustic sets to full folk bands. No tickets, no nonsense — just turn up, grab a drink, and enjoy. Children and babies absolutely welcome at early sessions.

Tuesday

Open Mic Night

Sign up at the bar from 7pm. All acoustic welcome. Regular faces and first-timers alike.

Friday

Live Band Night

A different act every week — folk, blues, pop and beyond. Kicks off at 8pm. Dancing encouraged.

Sunday

Acoustic Sunday Afternoons

Gentle live music from 2pm while you enjoy your Sunday roast or cream tea. Perfect for families.

Annual highlights

Events throughout the year

From community cream teas raising money for great local causes, to seasonal celebrations that the whole village looks forward to.

December

Annual Christmas Party

The biggest night of our year. Mulled wine, mince pies, live music, rider displays from our friends at the stables, and a visit from Father Christmas himself. Dressing up is encouraged — fancy dress prizes for the little ones.

Every season

Charity Cream Tea Days

On selected Sundays throughout the year, all proceeds from our legendary cream teas go directly to our chosen local charity — including the Riding for the Disabled Association South West Cornwall Group. Come for the scones, stay for the cause.

Year-round

Community Celebrations

Prize nights, fund-raisers, village quiz evenings, team gatherings — if your community group needs a warm, welcoming space for a celebration, we're your pub. Talk to us about hiring the venue.

December

Children's Nativity & Christmas

Every December we celebrate with the little ones — a Christmas nativity, carols, mince pies and a very special appearance from Father Christmas. Babies and toddlers in their Christmas finest very welcome.

Community at heart

More than a pub — a meeting place

The Old Mill Inn has always been about more than a pint. We're proud to support our local community, including organising events that bring together all generations — from the youngest babies to our oldest regulars.

In recent years we've been involved in hosting "Tea with a Pony" afternoons for local care home residents, supporting the Riding for the Disabled charity, and raising money for causes that matter to this village.

If you'd like to organise a community event with us, we'd love to hear from you.
