A little corner of Cornwall

Pull up a chair. You're among friends.

The Old Mill Inn has been the beating heart of our village for generations. Whether you're after a proper Sunday roast, a quiet pint in the garden, or a night of live music with the whole family in tow — this is your place.

We cook from scratch, pour a generous measure, and always keep the kettle on. Cream teas served daily. High chairs always ready. Dogs given a very warm welcome at the bar.

All ages welcome

Babies, toddlers & tiny humans — you're all very welcome

We firmly believe the best village pub is one where every generation feels at home. That means a full baby-changing room, high chairs at every table, a dedicated little ones' menu, and a garden designed for explorers aged 0 and up.

No shushing, no eye-rolling — just a warm welcome, a big smile, and perhaps a complimentary biscuit for the grown-ups who made it out of the house.

Life at the Mill

A glimpse inside

Outdoors

The Sunny Garden

Our beer garden is genuinely, gloriously sunny — and the perfect setting for a long, lazy afternoon. With the Cornish countryside on the doorstep, there's nowhere better to sit back with a cold drink and let the world slow down a little.

We grow herbs and edible flowers for our kitchen right here in the garden, and in summer the borders burst into colour. Kids can roam, dogs can stretch, and grown-ups can breathe.
