Food & Drink

Everything on our menu is cooked from scratch using Cornish produce. Simple, honest, delicious — and always with a warm smile on the side.

Our kitchen

Cooked from scratch. Always.

Our head chef has been cooking pub food in Cornwall for over twenty years, and the philosophy hasn't changed: use what's good and local, cook it properly, and don't mess about with it too much.

From slow-braised stews on a grey October afternoon to a cream tea in the garden with the sun on your face — we cook for the season and for the moment.

Cream Teas & Afternoon Tea

A proper Cornish cream tea: freshly baked scones, generous amounts of clotted cream, and strawberry jam — plus a pot of proper leaf tea (or a cheeky glass of something fizzy if you'd prefer).

Served every day from 2pm to 5pm. Available for groups of 6 or more by advance booking. On selected Sundays, cream tea proceeds go to our local Riding for the Disabled group.
