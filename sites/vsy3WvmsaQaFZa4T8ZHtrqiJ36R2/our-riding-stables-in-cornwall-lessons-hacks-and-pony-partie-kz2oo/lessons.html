Riding Lessons

For All Ages & Abilities

Learn to Ride in Beautiful Cornwall

✦

Our riding lessons are designed to nurture confidence, build skill, and ignite a lifelong love of horses. From tiny tots on their first pony to adults returning to the saddle, every rider receives the same dedicated, professional instruction.

All lessons are delivered by our BHS-qualified instructors in a safe, structured environment. We match each rider carefully with the most suitable horse or pony to ensure the very best experience.
