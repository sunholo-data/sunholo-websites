What could be more enchanting than spending your birthday with beautiful ponies? Our pony parties are lovingly crafted to create a truly special experience for children aged 3–12, with all the magic that only horses can bring.

Questions?

Party FAQs

  • Q
    How far in advance should I book? We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks ahead, especially for weekend parties and during school holidays when demand is highest.
  • Q
    Can parents stay and watch? Absolutely — parents are warmly welcomed and encouraged to stay for the whole party. We provide comfortable seating area near the arena.
  • Q
    What should children wear? Long trousers and closed-toe shoes are essential. We suggest a warm, waterproof layer as it's Cornwall — layers are always a good idea!
  • Q
    Can children with no experience take part? Yes! Our pony parties are designed for children of all experience levels, including complete beginners. Our ponies are perfectly suited to first-timers.
  • Q
    Can we bring our own cake & decorations? Of course! You are welcome to bring your own birthday cake and decorations. We can suggest a suitable area at the stables.
