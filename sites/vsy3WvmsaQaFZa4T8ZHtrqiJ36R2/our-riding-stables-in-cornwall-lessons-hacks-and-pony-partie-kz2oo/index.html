Our Story

A Passion for Horses, Rooted in Cornwall

✦

Our stables have been a cherished part of the Cornish community for years. We believe that horses have a unique power to inspire, heal, and delight — and we're dedicated to sharing that magic with everyone who visits.

Our team of experienced, caring instructors and friendly, well-schooled ponies and horses ensure every visit is safe, joyful, and truly memorable. From tiny tots to adults rediscovering their love of riding, all are welcome here.
