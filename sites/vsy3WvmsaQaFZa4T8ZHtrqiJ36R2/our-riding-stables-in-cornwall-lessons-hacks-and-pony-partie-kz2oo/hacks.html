Discover Cornwall on Horseback

The Most Beautiful Way to See Cornwall

✦

There is no finer way to experience Cornwall's magical landscape than from the back of a horse. Our guided hacks take you through ancient bridle paths, lush meadows, and rolling countryside that simply cannot be reached on foot or by car.

Each hack is led by an experienced guide who knows our routes intimately and will ensure your ride is both safe and spectacular. All horses are chosen for their calm, steady temperament on the trail.

Hacking Through Cornwall

Before You Go

Practical Information

  • ✓
    What to Wear Long trousers (jodhpurs or close-fitting trousers), sturdy boots with a small heel, and layers for Cornwall's changeable weather.
  • ✓
    Helmets We provide BSI-approved riding helmets. You are welcome to bring your own if correctly approved.
  • ✓
    Weight & Age Hacks are suitable for riders aged 12+ and up to 15 stone (95 kg) for the welfare of our horses.
  • ✓
    Experience Required A basic ability to walk and trot confidently is recommended for all hacks beyond our introductory option.
  • ✓
    Group Size We keep groups small (max 8) to ensure a personalised, safe, and enjoyable experience for every rider.
  • ✓
    Booking Advance booking is essential. We recommend booking at least two weeks ahead, especially during peak season.
  • ✓
    Cancellation We ask for 48 hours' notice for cancellations. Full details provided at time of booking.
  • ✓
    Weather We ride in all but the most extreme weather — Cornwall's drizzle is part of the adventure!
