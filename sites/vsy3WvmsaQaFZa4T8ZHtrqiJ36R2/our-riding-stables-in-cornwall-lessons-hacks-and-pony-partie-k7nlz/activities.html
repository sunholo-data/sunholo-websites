Our Activities

From therapeutic RDA sessions to seasonal celebrations, we have something for everyone at Old Mill Stables.

Therapeutic riding

RDA Riding Lessons

Our riding lessons are the heart of what we do. As an affiliated RDA group, we provide specialist riding sessions for children and adults with a wide range of physical, cognitive and sensory disabilities.

Each session is carefully planned by our trained RDA coaches to meet individual needs. Riders are supported by our team of dedicated side-walkers and leaders, ensuring a safe, positive and rewarding experience every time.

Our calm, specially trained ponies and horses are at the centre of it all — patient, gentle and responsive to every rider.

Explore Cornwall

Hacks & Escorted Rides

Experience the beauty of the Cornish countryside from the saddle. Our escorted hacks take you through the stunning landscapes surrounding Lelant Downs — woodlands, fields and breathtaking views that only Cornwall can offer.

Our hacks are suitable for a range of abilities, from confident beginners to more experienced riders. All rides are led by our knowledgeable team who know the local terrain and put your comfort and safety first.

Whether it is your first time on a horse or you ride regularly, a hack at Old Mill Stables is an unforgettable way to see Cornwall.

Celebrations & fun

Pony Parties & Seasonal Events

Make your child's birthday truly special with a pony party at Old Mill Stables! Our pony parties are tailored to your group, creating magical memories for children of all ages and abilities.

Throughout the year we also host a wonderful range of seasonal events — our much-loved Christmas celebration in the indoor arena is a highlight of the calendar, complete with festive fun, fancy dress, and even a nativity re-enactment.

These events bring our riders, volunteers and wider community together in celebration, and they are open to all — whether or not you are a regular rider with us.

Community outreach

Tea with a Pony

One of our most loved outreach initiatives, “Tea with a Pony” invites elderly and care home residents to come and spend time with our gentle ponies over a cup of tea and home-baked treats.

The sessions have been a wonderful success — the benefits of gentle animal interaction for wellbeing, particularly for elderly people, are well documented, and the joy on participants' faces speaks for itself.

Our sessions were featured in the local Cornish press, highlighting the heartwarming impact they have had on care home residents in the St Ives area.

We welcome enquiries from care homes, sheltered housing, community groups and supported living organisations. Sessions are bespoke to your group's needs and all our ponies are carefully selected for their calm temperament.

Support us

Fundraising & community events

As a charitable RDA group, we rely on the generosity of our community to keep our work going. We hold regular fundraising events throughout the year, including our popular Cream Tea at local venues.

All proceeds go directly to the Riding for the Disabled South West Cornwall Group, helping us to maintain our horses and ponies, fund training for coaches and volunteers, and keep our sessions affordable and accessible.

If you would like to support us, attend one of our events, or help organise fundraising, we would love to hear from you.
