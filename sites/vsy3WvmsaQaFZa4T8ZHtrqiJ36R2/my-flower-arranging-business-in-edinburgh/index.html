About the Studio

Flowers arranged
with intention

Every arrangement begins with a conversation. We take time to understand your space, your occasion, and the feeling you want to create — then we bring it to life through carefully chosen flowers, foliage, and form.

Based in Edinburgh, we create bespoke floral arrangements for homes, weddings, corporate events, and special occasions throughout Scotland. Our work is rooted in a love of seasonal floristry — using blooms that are at their most beautiful and vibrant.

Whether you need a single statement piece, a recurring subscription arrangement, or full floristry for a wedding or event, we approach every commission with the same care and attention to craft.
