Competitions & Events

Celebrating the achievements of our riders and the generosity of our local community.

Competitions & Success

Our riders compete at County, Regional, and National levels with great success. These events provide a platform for our participants to showcase their skills, build confidence, and experience the thrill of achievement in a supportive environment.

Annual Celebrations

We host annual shows and festive holiday events to celebrate our community's achievements. From Christmas gatherings to summer showcases, these events bring together riders, families, and volunteers in a spirit of joy and friendship.

Support Our Mission

From dog shows to country fairs, our fundraising events keep our arena open and our ponies fed. Every penny counts toward providing therapy for those who need it most. We rely on the dedication of our community to ensure our services remain accessible to all.
