Welcome to the South West Cornwall Group of the Riding for the Disabled Association. Based at Old Mill Stables, we provide therapy, fitness, and enjoyment to people with disabilities. Registered Charity No. 1073713.

A Legacy of Care

Founded in 1976, our group has grown from a small school project to a vital community resource. From our covered arena to our specialized mounting facilities, we ensure that everyone, regardless of ability, can experience the joy of horsemanship.

We focus on the individual, providing a supportive environment where physical and emotional milestones are celebrated every single day.
