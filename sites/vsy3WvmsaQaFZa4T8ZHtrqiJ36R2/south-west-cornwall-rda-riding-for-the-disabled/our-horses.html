The Real Heroes

Our horses are more than just animals; they are therapists, companions, and the heart of our community. Each one is chosen for their gentle nature and unique personality.

"Meet Jake, our 'round barrel naughty hedgerow nibbler,' and Max, a willful but clever lad who has been a grounding force for our riders for nearly a decade."

Steady & Kind

Our ponies undergo rigorous training to ensure they are comfortable with mounting ramps, side-walkers, and the varied needs of our riders.

Patient Teachers

A horse's rhythmic movement helps improve core strength and coordination, but it is the bond between rider and horse that creates the most magic.

A Sensory Experience

Beyond riding, our horses provide vital sensory feedback during our 'Tea with a Pony' sessions, offering comfort through touch and presence.

Caring for our Team

The welfare of our horses is our top priority. They live at Old Mill Stables where they receive expert care, regular veterinary check-ups, and plenty of time in the fields to just "be horses" when they aren't working with our participants.
