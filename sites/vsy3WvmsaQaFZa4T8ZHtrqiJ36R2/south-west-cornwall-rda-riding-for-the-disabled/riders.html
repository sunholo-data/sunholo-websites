Ride with Us

RDA can improve physical health, mobility, dexterity, and mental health. We welcome riders of all ages and abilities to experience the unique bond between horse and human.

Tea with a Pony

Our 'Tea with a Pony' sessions provide a sensory-rich, low-impact way to interact with our horses. It is especially popular with our elderly participants and those living with dementia, offering a calm and festive environment for social connection.

These sessions allow for gentle grooming, quiet observation, and sharing stories over a cup of tea, bringing the joy of the stables to those who may no longer be able to ride.
