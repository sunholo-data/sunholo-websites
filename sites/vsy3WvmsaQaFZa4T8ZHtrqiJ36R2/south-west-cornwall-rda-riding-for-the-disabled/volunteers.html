The Heart of Our Group

Volunteers are at the heart of everything we do. Without you, we could not provide this brilliant experience for our riders. Join our community and make a real difference.

Sue's Journey

"I was amazed at the difference RDA made to SEN children. The joy was palpable. I have seen a student progress from being unable to maintain her weight to holding herself upright unaided. It is truly rewarding to witness such growth."

— Sue Williams, Volunteer

How to Get Involved

No horse experience is necessary! We provide full training for side-walkers, leaders, and admin roles. Whether you have a few hours a week or a few hours a month, there is a place for you here.

Side-Walking

Walk alongside our riders to provide physical support and encouragement during their sessions.

Leading

For those with some horse experience, lead our ponies through the lessons under the instructor's guidance.

Admin & Events

Help us behind the scenes with fundraising, organizing events, or managing our daily operations.
