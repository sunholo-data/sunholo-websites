About me

Hi, I'm your local
flower artist!

Based in Gothenburg, I create joyful, unique flower arrangements for every occasion — big or small. From romantic bouquets to vibrant table centrepieces, each arrangement is crafted by hand with the freshest seasonal blooms.

I love bringing colour, life, and a little bit of magic into people's days through flowers. Whether it's for a birthday, wedding, dinner party, or just because — there's always a reason to have beautiful blooms around.
