Our Story

A modern art gallery for flowers, rooted in the landscapes of Spain.

Rooted in Community

Bloom & Line began as a small project focused on bringing joy to the community. Today, we maintain those roots through our volunteer programs and local sourcing initiatives throughout Spain. We believe that true luxury lies in the connection between the earth, the artisan, and the final arrangement.

The Hands Behind the Blooms

Our team is a small collective of artists and horticulturalists dedicated to the craft of modern floristry. We prioritize rhythm, balance, and the architectural integrity of every stem.
