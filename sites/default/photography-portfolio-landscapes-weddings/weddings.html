Questions

Wedding FAQ

Common questions can cover coverage hours, travel, second shooters, image delivery, editing style, and how to feel comfortable in front of the camera.

How many hours of coverage are available?

Coverage can be tailored to your day, from shorter celebrations to full-day storytelling that includes preparations, ceremony, portraits, and evening atmosphere.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes. Local and destination weddings can both be accommodated. Travel details, timing, and any related costs are discussed clearly during the enquiry stage.

Can a second photographer be included?

For larger celebrations or when extra coverage is helpful, a second shooter can be added to capture additional angles, guest reactions, and parallel moments.

When will we receive our images?

A preview selection is typically shared soon after the wedding, followed by a fully edited online gallery delivered within the agreed turnaround period.

What is your editing style?

The editing approach is clean, timeless, and restrained — true-to-moment tones, refined contrast, and a consistent finish designed to age beautifully.

We feel awkward in front of the camera. Is that okay?

Absolutely. Most couples say this at first. The approach is calm and lightly guided, creating space for natural interaction rather than forced posing.
