What We Do

From intimate weddings to weekly home subscriptions, we offer a range of floral services tailored to your needs. Each stem is chosen to bring a bit of the Scottish wild into your world.

Home Subscriptions

Enjoy seasonal, hand-tied bouquets delivered to your doorstep in Edinburgh every week or month. A gift to yourself that keeps on blooming.

Bespoke Gifting

One-off arrangements for birthdays, anniversaries, or 'just because'. Tell us your story and we'll translate it into petals.

Corporate Styling

Bring the calming influence of nature into your workspace, hotel, or restaurant with our tailored floral installations.

Weddings & Events

We specialize in wild, natural styling for weddings across Scotland. Let us bring your vision to life with textures and tones that reflect your unique personality. From bridal bouquets to full venue transformations, we focus on the organic beauty of the season.

Workshops & Community

Join us for our seasonal flower pressing and arranging workshops. We believe in the healing power of working with your hands and the joy of creating something beautiful alongside your neighbors. Perfect for hen parties, team building, or a quiet afternoon of self-care.
