Rooted in Community

Founded by a local volunteer with a passion for nature, Bloom & Briar started as a small project to bring color to our local neighborhood. Today, we remain committed to our roots, supporting local causes and fostering community spirit through every stem.

In the heart of Edinburgh, we believe that flowers are more than just decoration; they are a language of care, a bridge between neighbors, and a celebration of the wild Scottish earth.
