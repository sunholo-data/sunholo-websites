Visit us

Our collective is based in Grindelwald, in the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland. Visits to the cellar can be arranged by appointment — we welcome cheese lovers, restaurateurs, and wholesale buyers.

About the collective

Five farming families making raw milk cheese at 2000m altitude using traditional copper vat methods. Gruyère, Raclette, and seasonal specialities crafted with mountain patience.
