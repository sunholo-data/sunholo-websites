Our Story

Five families,
one vision

Five farming families in Grindelwald have pooled their herds to create exceptional Gruyère and Raclette using traditional copper vat methods. What began as neighbourly cooperation has become a celebrated cooperative, recognised for cheeses of extraordinary depth and character.

Our animals graze on the rich alpine meadows above 2000 metres, where the diversity of mountain herbs and flowers gives the raw milk its distinctive complexity. No shortcuts, no pasteurisation — only the honest transformation of remarkable milk into cheese worth waiting for.

Every wheel that leaves our cellar has been turned, washed, and tasted by the hands that made it. We age with patience, because great cheese cannot be hurried.

Each variety reflects the season, the pasture, and the craft of its makers.

01

Alpine Gruyère

12-month aged

Our flagship. Aged for a full year in our stone cellar, this Gruyère develops a complex, nutty flavour with sweet caramel notes and a smooth, supple paste. Made in copper vats from the morning's first milking.

02

Raclette Wheel

Semi-hard · Melting cheese

A full wheel of traditional Raclette, washed in brine and aged to perfection. Creamy, slightly pungent, and irresistibly meltable — made for long winter evenings around the fire.

03

Fresh Tomme

Young · Mild

A young, gentle cheese with a thin natural rind and milky, lactic interior. Ready within weeks of making, the Fresh Tomme captures the pure taste of alpine milk at its most delicate.

04

Herb-Crusted Mutschli

Seasonal · Hand-rolled

A small, round alpine cheese hand-rolled in a blend of wild mountain herbs — thyme, rosemary, and dried meadow flowers. A seasonal speciality that brings the mountain pasture directly to the table.
