Find the studio

The atelier is nestled in the winding streets of Alfama, Lisbon's oldest and most characterful neighbourhood. Just a short tram ride from Praça do Comércio — look for the blue-tiled doorway.

About Maria

A third-generation azulejo artist, Maria has been teaching the art of hand-painted Portuguese ceramic tiles for over fifteen years. She offers workshops in English, Portuguese, and French.
