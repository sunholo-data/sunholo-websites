Our Story

Three generations,
one art

Maria is a third-generation tile artist whose family has painted azulejos in Alfama for over seventy years. What began as her grandmother's small workshop has grown into a beloved atelier where visitors from around the world discover the joy of hand-painted Portuguese ceramics.

The tradition of azulejo — from the Arabic al-zulayj, meaning "polished stone" — has shaped Portugal's soul for five centuries. Every blue-and-white panel that adorns Lisbon's facades tells a story. In Maria's sun-drenched studio, you become part of that living tradition.

Whether you join a two-hour introduction or spend a full day mastering the craft, you'll leave with a tile that is entirely and uniquely yours — a piece of Lisbon to carry home.

Workshops & Classes

Every tile tells
your story

Choose the experience that fits your time and curiosity — from a first brush stroke to a complete commissioned piece.

01

Intro Workshop

2 hours · All levels welcome

The perfect introduction to azulejo painting. Learn the fundamentals of tile preparation, cobalt pigment mixing, and the classic geometric motifs that define Portuguese ceramic art. You'll leave with a finished tile ready to hang.

02

Full-Day Masterclass

Full day · Intermediate & above

A deep immersion into the art of azulejo. Explore historic pattern libraries, practice the traditional wet-on-wet cobalt technique, and complete a multi-tile composition under Maria's personal guidance. Small groups of six maximum.

03

Private Group Sessions

Flexible · Groups of 4–16

A joyful, creative experience for hen parties, birthday celebrations, team building, or simply a group of curious travellers. Fully tailored to your group's pace and interests — Maria brings warmth, storytelling, and very good Lisbon wine.

04

Custom Tile Commissions

Bespoke · 2–6 week turnaround

Commission a bespoke panel for your home, restaurant, or as a treasured gift. Maria works with you to design and hand-paint tiles that honour the azulejo tradition while reflecting your personal vision — each piece signed and unique.
