Rooted in Edinburgh

A journey through petals, stems, and the wild beauty of Scotland.

A Small Passion Project with Deep Roots

Founded in the heart of the capital, Bloom & Thistle began as a small passion project inspired by the untamed beauty of the Scottish Highlands and the charming cobblestone streets of Edinburgh.

Today, we are proud to be a vibrant part of the Edinburgh community, supporting local events and bringing smiles to our neighbors' faces through the delicate, expressive language of flowers. Every bouquet we craft is a tribute to the landscape we call home.

Whether we are teaching the art of the perfect wreath or styling a grand celebration, our philosophy remains the same: nature's beauty is meant to be shared.
