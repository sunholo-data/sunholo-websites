What We Offer

Tailored floral services for every occasion, rooted in the wild beauty of Edinburgh.

Wedding Florals

Bespoke bridal bouquets and venue styling that captures your unique love story with seasonal, organic textures.

Local Delivery

Fresh, seasonal blooms delivered daily across the city of Edinburgh. A touch of nature for your home or a gift for a loved one.

Workshops

Learn the art of floral design in our cozy studio. From hand-tied bouquets to seasonal wreaths, we share our passion with you.

Event Styling

We specialize in creating atmospheric floral installations for local events, community gatherings, and private parties. Our work often features in local festivals and seasonal celebrations across the capital.

Whether it's a small intimate dinner or a large corporate gala, we bring the same level of care and organic Scottish charm to every project.
